(Within) — Between. is the thesis exhibition for the 2016 graduating class of Pratt’s Graduate Communications Design Program.

Opening
Friday May 13th, 2016 6 - 9pm
On view May 13th - May 20th
Gallery open M-F 10am - 6pm
Location Pratt Manhattan, 7th Floor Gallery 144 W. 14th Street, New York, NY 10011
Directions
Join us this May 12th – 20th for exhibition produced by Pratt Institute’s Graduate Communications Design Department. The exhibition features eighteen original works and celebrates the connections found within and between the varied, yet united, work of communications designers. The opening reception, which will take place from 6-8pm on Friday, May 13th at 144 W14th Street, 7th floor, is open to the public and will offer light refreshments.
